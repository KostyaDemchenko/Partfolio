About

More About Me.

Let me to introduce myself; my name is Konstantin, and I am a Front-End/Web Developer hailing from the vibrant city of Kharkiv. At the age of 20, I bring to the table a year's worth of experience in the field, marked by a continuous pursuit of excellence and a commitment to staying abreast of industry trends.

My journey in the realm of development began as a Web Designer, a role that equipped me with a keen eye for design intricacies and proficiency in tools like Figma, Photoshop, and Illustrator. This background has proved invaluable in grasping project nuances swiftly, enhancing my effectiveness as a developer.

In terms of professional experience, I have dedicated approximately a year to honing my skills as a Front-End developer. Further details regarding my tenure can be found in the enclosed resume. Beyond technical prowess, I pride myself on attributes such as reliability, punctuality, resilience under pressure, adaptability, and strong interpersonal skills. These qualities, I believe, position me as a valuable addition to any team or project.

Previously, my technological toolkit encompassed HTML, CSS, JS/JQuery. However, in my quest for growth and efficiency, I have transitioned to a more robust stack, currently leveraging TS/JS Next, React, VUE 3, CSS/SCSS/Tailwind, along with several other JavaScript libraries.
